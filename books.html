Thoreau & Me in the Finger Lakes

There is a deep-seated connection between humans and the natural world. It is a timeless bond that is now more essential to our health and well-being than ever before. Thoreau believed that by immersing ourselves in nature we can lead happier, healthier lives. Modern science now agrees.

Thoreau & Me is a spiritual journey across the Finger Lakes country, where the reader will learn how connect with nature in remarkably powerful ways.

Lost Pond

November 3, 1928
The Canadian Flyer thunders north. But when the pride of the Adirondack Railway and Steamship Company arrives at Montreal’s Central Station shortly after 3 a.m. its lone passenger –his identity a closely guarded secret –has vanished. Days later the railroad’s imperious owner puts a pistol to his head and squeezes the trigger. The shocking suicide of Erik von Koenig and the disappearance of his vast fortune will remain a baffling mystery ...until a century later when a zany band of North Country characters, led by a phantomlike Adirondack hermit, battle a disgraced U.S. senator and the Russian mob to save the Adirondack community of Lost Pond.

The Raven

2107 A.D.
THE FINAL DAYS OF THE ADIRONDACK PARK.
In exchange for billions in defaulted bonds, the bankrupt State of New York is about to transfer four million acres of Adirondack Park land to a development consortium of Chinese and Saudi banks. Catastrophic climate change and collapse of the debt driven U.S. economy have devastated the dream that was America. But when a twenty-second-century adventurer discovers he is lost in the Adirondacks— and it is 1832—there is hope that America has one last chance to get it right, before everything goes terribly wrong.

Spirit Wolf

Nine-year-old Jason Quinn is blind. While visiting a remote Adirondack lodge, he is spellbound by the haunting call of a phantom white wolf. It is a wild, seductive cry only he can hear. A grizzled mountain guide, a Mohawk holy man and an angelic wandered lead Jason on a mystical vision quest. SPIRT WOLF reaches a thundering climax Christmas Day when the call of the wild flows from the mountains and surrounds Jason with its magic.
