PRESS RELEASE
May 2016

THOREAU & ME IN THE FINGER LAKES
Finding Nature’s Path to a Healthier, Happier Life

THE BOOK
Henry David Thoreau believed “we can never have enough of nature.” There is an innate connection between humans and the natural world. It is a timeless bond that is now more essential to our health and well-being than ever before. Thoreau and Me in the Finger Lakes awakens the reader’s spirit to a deepened awareness of nature and peaceful mindfulness with all of life.

THE AUTHOR
Mark W. Holdren has been honored by the Associated Press and New York Newspaper Publishers Association for his feature writing. He is the author of three Adirondack novels and two non-fiction Finger Lakes works. Holdren resides in Upstate New York.

THE ILLUSTRATOR
Elaine Verstraete is an award-winning illustrator of children’s books. She holds a BFA with honors from Syracuse University and resides in the Finger Lakes region.

Thoreau and Me in the Finger Lakes is published by Powell Hill Press (www.powellhillpress.com)
